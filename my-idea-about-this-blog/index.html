My Idea About This Blog

Posted: Feb 7, 2016 Tags: Writing |

So what is this blog about and why is it here?

I started a little blog on my Raspberry Pi a while back. I used a few different tools, finally selecting Nikola. That worked well for a while, but I’m not on Linux on a laptop right now and it’s a lot of leg work to keep up. The charm has worn off.

Recently, I bought a MacBook and discovered Mars Edit. I also discovered I can have a static blog on github. With Olai, I can use Mars Edit and push this content to . Not a lot of leg work involved.

I can’t customize anything, so that’s why you’re seeing titles and you must click a link to read the articles. I suppose this makes sense in a mobile world with band width caps.

I don’t know right now what I will do with my existing content. I may just repost it with newer dates. It will give me a chance to play with Mars Edit and review what I have written and do a bit of weeding.

Going forward, this blog will be my place to write about anything which interests me. I find writing blog posts is a way for me to keep notes and perhaps these notes will be useful to others.
